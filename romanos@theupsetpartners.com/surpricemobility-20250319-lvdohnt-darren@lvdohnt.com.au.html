Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value provided, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of car types.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Long-Term Savings : Emphasize the cost-effectiveness of renting with Surprice compared to competitors, especially with low-cost operations and competitive rates.
  • Transparent Pricing : Assure the customer of no hidden fees, with clear terms and conditions.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Highlight the brand’s extensive network across multiple countries and its growing reputation.
  • Awards and Recognition : Mention awards like the "Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021" to showcase credibility and innovation.
  • Customer Support : Emphasize the 24/7 customer service and help center, ensuring customers feel supported.
  • Positive Reviews and Testimonials : Share testimonials or reviews from satisfied customers to build trust.
  • Professional Expertise : Highlight the skilled and passionate team behind the brand, ensuring high-quality service.

These arguments address the objections directly by focusing on affordability, transparency, and the brand’s reliability.
