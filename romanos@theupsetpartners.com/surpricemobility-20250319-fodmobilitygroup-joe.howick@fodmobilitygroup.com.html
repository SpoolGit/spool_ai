Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value provided, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of car types.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Cost Efficiency : Emphasize low operational costs and competitive rates, ensuring customers get the best deal.
  • Long-Term Savings : Explain how the service can save money in the long run, such as through reliable vehicles and minimal maintenance issues.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Proven Track Record : Reference awards like the "Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021" to showcase credibility and innovation.
  • Global Presence : Highlight the extensive network of franchises and operations in multiple countries, demonstrating reliability and scalability.
  • Customer Support : Stress the 24/7 customer service and help center, ensuring customers feel supported at all times.
  • Positive Reviews and Testimonials : Share testimonials or reviews from satisfied customers to build trust.
  • Transparency : Point to clear terms and conditions, privacy policies, and a commitment to customer satisfaction.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments can still effectively address the objections.
