Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, extensive training, and IT infrastructure) that make the offering cost-effective.
  • Low-Cost Operations : Emphasize how the franchise model ensures low operational costs, enabling competitive rates and higher profitability.
  • Return on Investment (ROI) : Showcase the potential for profit maximization through global sales networks and franchise support systems.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Proven Track Record : Reference the Gold Award for Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021 as evidence of the brand’s credibility and innovation.
  • Global Presence : Highlight the brand’s expansion across multiple countries and its established reputation in the car rental industry.
  • Support Systems : Stress the comprehensive training, 24/7 support, and IT infrastructure provided to franchisees, ensuring their success and building trust in the brand.

If the context doesn’t directly address these objections, the seller can focus on transparency , customer testimonials , and flexible pricing options to build trust and address cost concerns.
