Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections:

  1. Cost Concerns

    • Highlight the value-for-money proposition, emphasizing that the service offers optimal prices without compromising on quality.
    • Mention special offers and competitive rates that cater to budget-conscious customers.
    • Emphasize the low-cost operations that allow for cheaper car rental offerings compared to competitors.

  2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

    • Showcase the global reach and 24/7 service , demonstrating reliability and accessibility.
    • Highlight the quality of service , pleasant staff, and credibility as key features of the brand.
    • Point to the franchise opportunities and the brand’s growing reputation, which indicate trust and expansion.
    • Share customer testimonials or awards (e.g., recognition in Montenegro and Serbia) to build credibility.

These arguments address the objections directly by focusing on affordability, value, and trustworthiness.
