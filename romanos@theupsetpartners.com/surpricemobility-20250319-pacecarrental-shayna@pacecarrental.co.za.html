Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, multiple insurance options, and global reach) that justify the cost.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Long-Term Savings : Emphasize how the service can save money in the long run (e.g., low-cost operations, competitive rates, and efficient fleet management).

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Proven Track Record : Reference the brand’s awards, such as the Gold Award for Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021 , to establish credibility.
  • Global Presence : Highlight the extensive network of franchises and operations in multiple countries, showcasing reliability and scalability.
  • Customer Support : Stress the 24/7 customer service and professional support available to ensure a smooth experience.
  • Testimonials and Reviews : Share positive feedback from satisfied customers or partners to build trust.

These arguments address the objections directly and leverage the brand’s strengths to reassure potential customers.
