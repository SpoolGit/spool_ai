Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, multiple insurance schemes, and optional extras) that make the cost worthwhile.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Long-Term Savings : Emphasize how the service can save money in the long run (e.g., low-cost operations, competitive rates, and efficient fleet management).

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Showcase the brand’s extensive network across multiple countries and its growing reputation.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers.
  • Professional Support : Highlight the expertise of the team, 24/7 customer service, and the robust IT infrastructure that ensures reliability.
  • Transparency : Point to clear terms and conditions, privacy policies, and a commitment to data protection to build credibility.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments are still effective for addressing the objections.
