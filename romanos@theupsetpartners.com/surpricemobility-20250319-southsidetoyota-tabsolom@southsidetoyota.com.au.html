Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Emphasize the quality of service, 24/7 support, and competitive pricing compared to competitors.
  • Flexible Options : Highlight any discounts, promotions, or customizable plans that can reduce costs.
  • Long-Term Savings : Explain how the service can save money in the long run (e.g., fewer maintenance issues, better efficiency).

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Social Proof : Showcase positive reviews, testimonials, and the brand’s presence on social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.).
  • Transparency : Highlight the clear terms and conditions, privacy policy, and customer support options.
  • Global Presence : Mention the brand’s franchise opportunities and widespread operations in multiple countries, demonstrating reliability and scalability.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these general strategies can effectively address the objections.
