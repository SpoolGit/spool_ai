Objection Handling +

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Emphasize the competitive pricing and the value provided, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of vehicles.
  • Special Offers : Highlight ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Long-Term Savings : Explain how the service can save costs in the long run, such as through efficient fleet management or low operational fees.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Mention the extensive network of franchises and operations in multiple countries, showcasing reliability and scalability.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers to build credibility.
  • Transparency : Highlight the clear terms and conditions, privacy policies, and 24/7 customer support to reassure potential clients.
  • Brand Reputation : Reference awards, recognitions, or partnerships that demonstrate the brand’s trustworthiness and industry standing.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments can still effectively address the objections.
