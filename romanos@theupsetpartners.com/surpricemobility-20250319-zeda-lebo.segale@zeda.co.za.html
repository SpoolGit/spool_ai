Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

Cost Concerns

  1. Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, multiple insurance options, and global reach) that make the cost worthwhile.
  2. Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  3. Cost Efficiency : Emphasize the low-cost operations and competitive rates that ensure customers get the best deal.

Lack of Trust in the Brand

  1. Awards and Recognition : Reference the Gold Award for Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021 to establish credibility and innovation.
  2. Global Presence : Highlight the extensive network across multiple countries and popular destinations, showcasing reliability and experience.
  3. Customer Support : Stress the 24/7 service and professional team dedicated to customer satisfaction.
  4. Testimonials and Reviews : Encourage potential customers to check reviews or testimonials from satisfied users.

If the context doesn’t help, I would handle these objections by:

  • Cost Concerns : Offer flexible payment plans or highlight long-term savings.
  • Lack of Trust : Provide case studies, free trials, or money-back guarantees to build confidence.
