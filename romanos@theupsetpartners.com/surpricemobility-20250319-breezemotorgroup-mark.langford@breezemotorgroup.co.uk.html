Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value customers receive, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of car types.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Cost Efficiency : Emphasize low operational costs that allow for cheaper offerings compared to competitors.
  • Long-Term Savings : Explain how the service can save money in the long run, such as through reliable vehicles and minimal hidden fees.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Showcase the brand’s extensive network across multiple countries and its growing reputation.
  • Awards and Recognition : Reference awards like the "Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021" to establish credibility.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers.
  • Transparency : Highlight clear terms of use, privacy policies, and 24/7 customer support to build confidence.
  • Partnerships : Mention collaborations with well-known travel agencies and brokers to reinforce trust.

By addressing these objections with these arguments, the seller can effectively reassure potential customers.
