Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value customers receive, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of car types.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Cost Efficiency : Emphasize low operational costs and competitive rates compared to other brands.
  • Transparent Pricing : Assure customers there are no hidden fees, and all costs are clearly outlined upfront.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Highlight the brand’s expansion into multiple countries (e.g., Canada, Italy, Greece) and its growing reputation.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers.
  • Professional Support : Emphasize the 24/7 customer service and expert team available to assist with any issues.
  • Partnerships : Mention collaborations with trusted brokers, travel agencies, and global distribution systems.
  • Transparency : Point to clear terms of use, privacy policies, and customer support channels to build credibility.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments can still effectively address the objections.
