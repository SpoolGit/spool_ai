Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the quality of service offered, ensuring customers get the best value for their money.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions, discounts, or flexible payment options to make the service more affordable.
  • Long-Term Savings : Emphasize how the service can save costs in the long run (e.g., 24/7 support, low operational costs, and efficient fleet management).

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Showcase the brand’s extensive network and presence in multiple countries, demonstrating reliability and experience.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers to build credibility.
  • Transparency : Highlight the brand’s commitment to transparency, such as clear terms of use, privacy policies, and 24/7 customer support.
  • Partnerships : Mention partnerships with reputable organizations or franchises to reinforce trust.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments are still effective for addressing the objections.
