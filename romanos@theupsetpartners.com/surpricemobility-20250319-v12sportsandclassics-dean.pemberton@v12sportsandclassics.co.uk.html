Qualification Questions +

Based on the context provided, here are the 5 most relevant and important questions the seller should ask the prospect to determine if they are a good fit for Surprice Car Rentals' B2B offering, which includes franchise opportunities and partnerships with existing car rental companies:

  1. What is your current business model, and how do you see a partnership or franchise with Surprice Car Rentals enhancing your operations?
    • This helps assess the prospect's alignment with Surprice's offerings and their potential to benefit from the global sales network and brand.
  2. Do you have experience in the car rental or automotive industry, and if so, what has been your focus (e.g., used car sales, rentals, fleet management)?
    • This evaluates the prospect's industry expertise and whether they can leverage Surprice's training and support effectively.
  3. What are your goals for expansion or growth, and how do you envision leveraging Surprice’s IT infrastructure and global sales network to achieve them?
    • This identifies the prospect's ambitions and their ability to utilize Surprice's resources to scale their business.
  4. Are you looking for a franchise opportunity or a partnership, and what level of support or training would you require from Surprice to succeed?
    • This clarifies the prospect's needs and ensures Surprice can provide the necessary support, such as training, IT infrastructure, and operational guidance.
  5. What is your current customer base, and how do you plan to integrate Surprice’s services (e.g., online booking facilities, competitive rates) to enhance their experience?
    • This assesses the prospect's ability to integrate Surprice's offerings into their existing operations and improve customer satisfaction.

These questions are designed to uncover the prospect's business needs, industry experience, and alignment with Surprice's franchise and partnership model.

These arguments address the objections directly by focusing on affordability, transparency, and trustworthiness.
