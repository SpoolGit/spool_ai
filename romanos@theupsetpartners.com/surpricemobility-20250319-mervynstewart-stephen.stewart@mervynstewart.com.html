Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

Cost Concerns

  1. Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, multiple insurance schemes, and global reach) that make the cost worthwhile.
  2. Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  3. Long-Term Savings : Emphasize how the service can save money in the long run through efficient operations and low-cost management.

Lack of Trust in the Brand

  1. Global Presence : Point out the extensive network of franchises and operations in multiple countries, showcasing reliability and scalability.
  2. Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or case studies from satisfied customers to build credibility.
  3. Transparency : Highlight the company’s clear terms and conditions, privacy policy, and 24/7 customer support to demonstrate trustworthiness.
  4. Awards and Recognition : Mention any awards or recognitions the brand has received to reinforce its reputation.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments can still be effective in addressing the objections.
