Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value-added services (e.g., 24/7 support, extensive network, and low-cost operations) that make the offering cost-effective.
  • Long-Term Savings : Emphasize how the franchise model ensures low operational costs, leading to higher profitability over time.
  • Flexible Pricing Options : Mention any financing or payment plans available to ease initial financial burdens.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Proven Track Record : Reference the Gold Award for Franchise Business Model Innovation 2021 and the brand’s 12-year history of success.
  • Global Presence : Highlight the extensive network across multiple countries, showcasing the brand’s reliability and scalability.
  • Support and Training : Stress the comprehensive training, IT infrastructure, and 24/7 support provided to franchisees, ensuring their success.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share success stories or testimonials from existing franchisees to build credibility.

If the context doesn’t provide enough information, these arguments can still be effective in addressing the objections.
