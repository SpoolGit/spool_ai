Objection Handling +

Here are the most relevant arguments the seller can use to overcome the objections of "Cost concerns" and "Lack of trust in the brand" :

1. Cost Concerns

  • Value for Money : Highlight the competitive pricing and the value provided, such as 24/7 service, multiple insurance options, and a wide range of car types.
  • Special Offers : Mention ongoing promotions or discounts that make the service more affordable.
  • Cost Efficiency : Emphasize low operational costs and competitive rates compared to other brands.
  • Transparent Pricing : Assure customers there are no hidden fees, and all costs are clearly outlined upfront.

2. Lack of Trust in the Brand

  • Global Presence : Highlight the brand’s expansion into multiple countries (e.g., Canada, Italy, Greece) and its growing network of franchises.
  • Customer Testimonials : Share positive reviews or awards (e.g., recognition in Montenegro and Serbia) to build credibility.
  • 24/7 Support : Stress the availability of round-the-clock customer service for assistance and reliability.
  • Professional Expertise : Mention the skilled team and comprehensive training programs that ensure high-quality service.
  • Online Presence : Point to the brand’s active engagement on social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) and its professional website.

By addressing these objections with clear, evidence-based arguments, the seller can build trust and justify the cost effectively.
