What Digip Should Probe (Relevant Qualification Questions)
A. Portfolio Management

"How do you currently manage trademarks across your products and markets?"

Goal: Understand reliance on law firms and lack of centralized overview.

B. Pain Points

"What's currently the most challenging part of trademark management for Theracon?"

Look for: Cost unpredictability, slow clearance, regulatory delays.

C. Monitoring & Enforcement

"How do you monitor for similar or conflicting names, especially in pharma-relevant classes?"

Goal: Identify dependence on Corsearch / Clarivate and manual review.

D. Renewal & Maintenance Control

"How do you ensure renewal deadlines aren't missed across jurisdictions?"

Look for: Law-firm reminders, no central dashboard.

E. Go-to-Market Registrations

"When launching a new product or licensing a therapy, how long does trademark clearance typically take?"

Goal: Surface delays impacting commercialization.

F. Internal Tech Maturity

"Do you use a platform to track trademarks, or is it mainly handled through advisors and email?"

Goal: Position Digip as simplification + control.

G. Vendor Satisfaction

"How satisfied are you with your current IP providers in terms of speed, clarity, and cost?"

Goal: Create opening for replacement or consolidation.
