What Matters to Wise (Strategic Motives)
A. Portfolio Management

"How do you currently manage your global trademark portfolio across all regions?"

Goal: Reveal fragmentation across Clarivate/Corsearch + law firms + local agents.

B. Pain Points

"What are the biggest challenges managing your trademark portfolio today?"

Look for: High cost, complexity, manual work, unclear visibility.

C. Monitoring & Enforcement

"How do you track infringements across markets today?"

Goal: Identify if watch services are outdated, reactive, or siloed.

D. Renewal & Maintenance Control

"How do you manage renewal deadlines across 150+ jurisdictions?"

Look for: Spreadsheet workflows, attorney reminders, lack of automation.

E. Go-to-Market Registrations

"What's your process when Wise launches a new product or enters a new country?"

Goal: Uncover slow, expensive clearance + multi-agent coordination.

F. Internal Tech Maturity

"Do you use digital tools/automation today, or mostly email + spreadsheets?"

Goal: Position Digip's centralized automation.

G. Vendor Satisfaction

"How satisfied are you with your current providers (e.g., Clarivate, Corsearch, MarkMonitor, external counsel)?"

Goal: Open door for cost, transparency, and speed comparison."
