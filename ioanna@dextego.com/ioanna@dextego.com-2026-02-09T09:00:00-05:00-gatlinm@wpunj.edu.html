Identified Pain Points

Coaching Bottlenecks: Faculty can't be everywhere at once; students only get 'episodic' coaching rather than continuous reinforcement.

Skill Decay: Students may learn frameworks in class but lose the 'muscle memory' of execution between semesters.

Subjectivity in Soft Skills: Difficulty in providing objective, data-driven feedback on 'human' traits like presence and trust.

Technological Gap: Ensuring the curriculum remains relevant as employers increasingly look for 'AI-literate' sales talent who understand behavioral intelligence.
