  2. Emphasize the free trial period (15 days) that allows them to test the full feature set without commitment
  3. Point out that the system eliminates need for multiple paid tools/services by consolidating everything in one platform
  4. Highlight the pay-as-you-go option at $129/job/month, which provides flexibility for small companies to scale up/down as needed

For "Current process works fine":

  1. Demonstrate how the platform enhances rather than replaces their existing methods - they can still use referrals and job boards but with better organization and reach
  2. Highlight the AI-powered features that automate manual tasks, allowing staff to focus on more strategic work
  3. Emphasize the expanded reach through 200+ job boards with one click posting, including premium visibility on LinkedIn and Indeed
  4. Point out that as they grow, having a structured hiring system in place will become increasingly important to maintain quality and consistency

The key is to position the software as an investment in efficiency and growth rather than just an expense, while showing how it enhances their current processes rather than completely changing them.
