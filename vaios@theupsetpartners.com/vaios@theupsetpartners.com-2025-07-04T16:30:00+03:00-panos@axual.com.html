Short Description:

Axual is a software development company based in Utrecht, Netherlands, founded in 2015 by CEO Jeroen van Disseldorp. The company employs around 46 people and specializes in an Enterprise Streaming Platform tailored for mid-size to large organizations. This platform enables real-time data streaming, allowing businesses to efficiently manage and process large volumes of data.

The platform offers various functionalities, including real-time customer interaction, process execution, partner integration, and sensor data processing. Built on Apache Kafka technology, Axual's solutions are ideal for applications such as real-time notifications, fraud detection, and predictive analytics. The company serves clients across multiple sectors, including finance, energy, public, and retail, with notable partnerships including Rabobank, de Volksbank, and Eneco. In 2022, Axual secured growth capital to enhance its real-time data streaming capabilities.
