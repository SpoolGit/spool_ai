Short Description:

SOFTONE is a prominent Greek software group that specializes in enterprise solutions. Established in 2002 and headquartered in Athens, the company is part of the Olympia Group and has grown through strategic acquisitions, creating a multi-brand technology ecosystem. With over 550 employees, SOFTONE operates in Greece, Romania, Cyprus, Bulgaria, and the UK, serving more than 75,000 enterprises and connecting over 350,000 companies through its B2B network.

The company offers a range of core solutions, including cloud-based and on-premise ERP and CRM systems, electronic invoicing, and supply chain automation tools. SOFTONE also provides professional accounting software and has a strong focus on industries such as retail, manufacturing, and logistics. Its SaaS ERP solutions are backed by ISO 27001-certified security protocols, ensuring reliable service for its clients. SOFTONE's recent acquisitions have further enhanced its capabilities, solidifying its position as a leader in Greece's technology market.

  1. Focus on your team's deep expertise and track record:

The key is to position yourself as an experienced partner who enhances their existing capabilities rather than disrupting them, while demonstrating relevant enterprise experience despite your company's size.
