Handling Objection

Overcoming Objections

1. Cost concerns for a small company (61 employees)
  • ROI focus: Emphasize how the solution delivers measurable returns that outweigh the investment - show concrete examples of cost savings or revenue increases
  • Scalable pricing: Highlight any tiered pricing options that make the solution accessible for smaller companies
  • Cost of inaction: Quantify what it costs the company to continue without your solution (missed opportunities, inefficiencies)
  • Success stories: Share case studies of similar-sized companies that achieved significant ROI
2. Integration challenges with existing AI sales tech
  • Seamless integration: Emphasize your solution's compatibility with common AI sales tools
  • Implementation support: Highlight the hands-on implementation assistance your team provides
  • Phased approach: Suggest a gradual implementation plan to minimize disruption
  • API flexibility: Discuss your solution's API capabilities that enable custom integrations
  • Complementary value: Show how your solution enhances rather than replaces their existing tech stack

The key is demonstrating tangible value that justifies the investment while making integration as frictionless as possible.
