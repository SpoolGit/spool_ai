The Institute of Entrepreneurship Development (iED) is a Greek non-profit research organization based in Larissa, Greece. Established in 2005, iED specializes in supporting entrepreneurship, managing EU projects, and fostering innovation ecosystems across Europe. The organization aims to empower entrepreneurs and organizations through expert guidance, funding solutions, and strategic partnerships.

iED operates as a leader in entrepreneurship research and digital innovation, collaborating with over 4,200 partner organizations throughout the EU and beyond. Its services include planning and implementing EU-funded projects, providing training and market access support for entrepreneurs, and developing digital tools for project management. iED has successfully implemented over 250 EU projects and is recognized as a Center of Excellence in European entrepreneurship. Its target clients include SMEs, startups, educational institutions, and public bodies seeking support in entrepreneurship and EU funding.
