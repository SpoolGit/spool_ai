Short Description:

Digip is a technology-driven company that specializes in intellectual property (IP) services, focusing on trademark management. It offers a comprehensive platform designed to assist businesses of all sizes in registering, protecting, and maintaining their trademarks globally, including in the US and EU markets.

The platform features scalable trademark management solutions that integrate advanced technology with legal expertise. Services include trademark application filing, automated trademark watching, initial legal assessments, and guaranteed trademark renewals. Key features include an AI-powered watch engine for alerts on potential conflicts, a centralized IP dashboard for portfolio management, and workflow automation for tracking deadlines. Digip operates on a subscription-based pricing model, providing predictable monthly costs and user-friendly access without requiring technical expertise. The company is committed to delivering strong customer support through various communication channels.

Overall, Digip provides an innovative approach to trademark management, enabling clients to maintain control over their trademark portfolios efficiently and cost-effectively.
