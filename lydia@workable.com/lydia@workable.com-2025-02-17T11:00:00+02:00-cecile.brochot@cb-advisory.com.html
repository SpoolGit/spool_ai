  • Focus on ROI: One customer (Alexander Dennis) saved over $300k in agency fees in first year
  • Position it as an investment that grows with them ("buy what you need and workable can grow with you")

2. Current manual process works fine:

  • Share specific metrics showing impact (e.g., Lethbridge School District reduced time-to-hire by 50%)
  • Highlight how automation frees up time from administrative tasks to focus on quality candidate interactions
  • Point out that in today's competitive market, moving quickly on good candidates is critical (per customer quote: "we put such importance on moving quickly because we know how competitive it is out there")
  • Emphasize ease of use with "straightforward, easy-to-use system" that requires minimal training to adopt

The key is focusing on tangible business outcomes and ROI rather than just features, while making the transition seem low-risk through the free trial option.
