Most relevant arguments:

  • Emphasize ROI: "Workable offers simple, fair pricing that scales with your business. Our customers see significant ROI through reduced time-to-hire and improved quality of hires."
  • Highlight the free trial: "We offer a 15-day free trial with no credit card required, allowing you to experience the full feature set before making any financial commitment."
  • Focus on efficiency gains: "The platform automates many manual recruitment tasks, reducing the administrative burden on your team and freeing them to focus on higher-value activities."
  • Stress the all-in-one solution: "Workable combines multiple recruitment tools in one platform, eliminating the need for several separate subscriptions and potentially reducing your overall technology spend."

2. Lack of perceived need or urgency for recruitment software

Most relevant arguments:

  • Emphasize competitive advantage: "Over 30,000 companies use Workable to find and hire top talent. Without modern recruitment tools, you risk losing qualified candidates to competitors who can move faster."
  • Highlight AI capabilities: "Our AI screening assistant and sourcing tools help you identify qualified candidates you might otherwise miss, giving you access to a wider talent pool."
  • Stress time savings: "Workable's automated workflows, interview scheduling, and collaborative tools dramatically reduce the time spent on administrative tasks in the hiring process."
  • Focus on candidate experience: "Today's candidates expect a smooth, professional application process. Workable helps you deliver this experience, preventing top talent from dropping out of your pipeline due to frustration."
  • Point to compliance benefits: "The platform helps ensure your hiring processes remain compliant with EEO/OFCCP regulations, reducing legal risks associated with manual recruitment processes."
