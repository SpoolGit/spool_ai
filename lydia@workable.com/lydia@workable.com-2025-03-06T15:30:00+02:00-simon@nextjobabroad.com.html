Objection Handling +

Based on the context and my expertise, here's how I would handle these objections for a small recruitment agency:

For Objection 1 - Cost/ROI:

  1. Focus on specific cost savings:
    • Highlight how the system reduces time-to-hire by 40 days on average
    • Emphasize 30% increased HR productivity through automation
    • Mention 100% removal of third-party tool costs by consolidating everything in one platform
    • Calculate ROI showing money saved on manual tasks and administrative work
  2. Stress scalability:
    • The system grows with their business
    • Pay only for what they need
    • No long-term commitment required with monthly plans

For Objection 2 - Current Process Works:

  1. Acknowledge their current process while highlighting pain points:
    • Manual processes take time away from revenue-generating activities
    • Self-scheduling features eliminate back-and-forth emails with candidates
    • Automated candidate sourcing expands reach without extra effort
  2. Focus on competitive advantage:
    • Access to 400M+ candidate profiles
    • AI-powered screening saves time evaluating candidates
    • Modern candidate experience gives them an edge over competitors
    • Structured interview tools ensure consistent evaluation

The key is showing how the solution helps them work smarter, not just differently, while delivering clear ROI through time and cost savings.
