Objection Handling +

Based on the context and putting myself in the seller's shoes, here are the most relevant counter-arguments for each objection:

  1. For "We already have existing payment/financing arrangements":

    • Emphasize that Copay is not a replacement but a complementary solution that offers zero-risk, high-yield returns
    • Highlight that unlike traditional factoring, this is a direct arrangement with clients that's "cheaper and easier to stop using" (as mentioned in the testimonial)
    • Point out that it improves supply chain relationships by creating a "win-win situation" rather than just being a financing arrangement

  2. For "Our current payment terms work fine":

    Key value propositions to emphasize:

    • Potential to generate "millions in savings" through dynamic discounting
    • Improved supply chain resilience by reducing vendor financial distress
    • No need for external funding or debt
    • Free 24/7 portal access for better visibility of payment and invoice data
    • Simple integration with existing systems (like COINS)

The key is to position the solution not just as a payment terms change, but as a strategic tool that benefits both parties while improving supply chain relationships and generating additional value through savings.
