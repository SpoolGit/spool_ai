  • No additional capital required - uses existing cash more efficiently to generate returns

The key is focusing on the low-risk, high-return nature of the solution while emphasizing it enhances rather than replaces existing processes. The value proposition combines immediate cost savings with strategic benefits like improved supplier relationships and supply chain stability.
