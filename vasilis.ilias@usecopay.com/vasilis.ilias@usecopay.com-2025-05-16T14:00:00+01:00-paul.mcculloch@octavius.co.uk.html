Handling Objection
Based on the context and putting myself in the seller's shoes, here are the most relevant counter-arguments for each objection:

For Objection 1 - "We already have existing solutions":

  • Unlike factoring, Copay doesn't involve external funding or debt - it works directly with your existing clients
  • The solution is more flexible and easier to stop using compared to factoring (as mentioned by one MD testimonial)
  • It creates a win-win situation by incentivizing early payments from clients rather than aggressive collection
  • Can be used alongside existing solutions as a complementary tool to optimize cash flow

For Objection 2 - "Not enough payment delays to justify":

  • The system is free to implement and only costs when actually used - so there's no downside to having it available
  • It provides value beyond just payment acceleration:
    • Real-time tracking dashboard for financials
    • 24/7 access to payment and invoice data
    • Improved relationship with clients through incentivized early payments
  • Can be used flexibly only when needed, unlike more rigid financing solutions
  • Helps build financial resilience for unexpected future delays or cash flow issues

The key is emphasizing flexibility, ease of implementation, and additional benefits beyond just payment acceleration.
