Handling Objection
Based on the context and taking the role of a seller, here's how I would handle these objections:

For Objection 1: "We already have existing payment/financing arrangements"

  • Emphasize that Copay is not a traditional financing arrangement but rather a dynamic discounting tool that creates a win-win situation
  • Point out that unlike factoring companies which are "expensive and hard to stop using," Copay offers flexibility with no external funding or debt
  • Highlight that it can work alongside existing arrangements while providing better terms and more control

For Objection 2: "We don't have cash available to pay suppliers early"

  • Clarify that this solution actually helps improve cash flow, not strain it
  • Explain that suppliers can choose when to accelerate payments based on their needs ("get paid on your terms")
  • Emphasize the free portal access and real-time tracking of invoices, allowing better financial management
  • Point out that it's a zero-risk solution that helps reduce supply chain financial distress

The key is to position Copay as a flexible, cost-effective tool that gives more control over cash flow rather than a traditional financing arrangement that ties up cash.
